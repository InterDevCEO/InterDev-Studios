Frequently Asked Questions

Are you experienced enough as a new company?

While we're new as a company, our team has solid technical skills and we're passionate about delivering quality work. We're building our portfolio and reputation with every project.

As a small team, we can be more flexible with timelines. Simple projects take 1-2 weeks, while complex ones may take 3-4 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance plans to keep your site updated and secure. We're committed to long-term relationships with our clients.

Absolutely! As a startup, we're flexible with pricing and can work with different budgets. We'll find the best solution for your needs and budget.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
